About Me

Hi! My name is Roger Zheng, and I’m a junior tennis player and coach. I am currently 14 years old and go to Alpharetta High School. I have always enjoyed and have been passionate about the sport ever since I first started playing at the age of six. Over the years, I’ve participated in many tennis activities and events, including USTA tournaments, ALTA leagues, and Chamblee High School junior varsity tennis team.

My love for the sport began with playing alongside my dad, who always supported me and made practicing enjoyable. Inspired by him, I strive to help every kid I coach experience tennis as a sport that is fun to learn, fun to play, and something to look forward to every day.

Tennis is a challenging sport with numerous obstacles and challenges, but with time and practice, it can become enjoyable. This is why I want to help other young players, like myself, improve their skills and boost their confidence on the court while still having fun.
